Macaroni and Tomato Juice

Description

Macaroni and tomato juice is a legendary dish that is easy to make, and easy to eat. While most people have never heard of it, I think it is amazing. My partner has never heard of it, but I put her on real quick.

Not much else can be said about the dish, though you'd be a fool to discount it. One slurp and guzzle, and you'll be tighter than a puzzle.

Ingredients

  • 1 Box Maccy
  • 1 Bottle Mater Juice

  • Peench of salt

Steps

  1. Salt and boil water
  2. Pour macaroni into pot
  3. Drain water after boil
  4. Pour mater juice into pot
  5. Add thanguh butter
  6. Add peench of salt
