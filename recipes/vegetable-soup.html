Vegetable Soup

Description

Vegetable soup truly is a testament to the old saying "throw it in, buddy, it'll work. I personally have extensive history with this glorious dish.

Using random cans of veggies, and a lil bituh mater juice, you can really rock the socks off your bump buddy. Go on, try it.

Ingredients

  • Can of corn
  • Can of peas
  • Can of potatoes
  • Mater juice
  • Thanguh butter

Steps

  1. Pour mater juice into pot on low heat
  2. Pour can of cone
  3. Pour can of peaz
  4. Pour can of taters
  5. Add thanguh
  6. serve
